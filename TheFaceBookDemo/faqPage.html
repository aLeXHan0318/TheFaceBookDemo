What is WWW.com?
WWW.com is an online directory that connects people through social networks.
How do you get our information?
Your school is not providing us with any information about you. All information and pictures are provided voluntarily by users.
How can I protect my privacy?
You can adjust your privacy settings to allow only certain people to view your info. You can also limit who can message you.
Can people from other schools join the site?
People from other schools can join, but they cannot view your profile unless you have added them as a friend.
What is the social net?
You social net is the group ofall users whose prvacy settings allow you to view their information. To make things more interesting, we also limit it to only users who have submitted pictures.
Why does the social net repeat people?
People from other colleges can join this website, but nobdy from another highschools can see our profile or social network unless you have listed them as your friend. Each highschool has its own subsection within the site where its members can view each other's profiles.
How do I search for something besides names?
You can either click the "Search all fields" button on the searchpage, or try the advanced search page.
If I reject someone, will they find out
No. When you reject someone, their friend request will leave your list of friendships to confirm, but they will not be notified. They also will not be able to send you another friend request for some amount of time, so to them, it will just seem as if you haven't confirmed their friendship yet.

Why is the visualization slow?
Every time you view a visualization, we need to figure out whether you have the appropriate previleges to see each person on the graph. This takes time. In addition, in order to maintain the overall performance of therest of the site, we have set up the site to process other requests with a higher priority than requests to generate visualitions.
How do I naviagate through the visualization?
Hold down 'Alt' while dragging to scroll, or right-click to zoom. We apoligzize for the difficulty of navigating the visualitions. We didn't make the SVG viewer; we just use it to bring you pretty pictures.
Can I change my name and password?
Yes -- you can request a name change and change your password on your my account page. For quality control purposes, we confirm all name chagnes before they take place. Password changes take effect immediately.
